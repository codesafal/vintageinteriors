Services

At Vintage Interior, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to bring your design visions to life. Our dedicated team of experienced designers is committed to creating spaces that reflect your unique style and enhance your lifestyle.

Explore our diverse array of services:




Embark on your design journey with a personalized consultation. Our experts will work closely with you to understand your preferences, lifestyle, and aspirations. From there, we'll craft a customized design plan that aligns with your vision.


Transform your house into a home with our residential design services. Whether you're looking to revamp a single room or undertake a complete home makeover, our designers bring creativity and functionality to every corner.


Elevate your business environment with our commercial design expertise. We specialize in creating functional and aesthetically pleasing workspaces that align with your brand identity and support the productivity of your team.


Optimize the layout and flow of your space with our space planning services. We carefully analyze your space to ensure efficient use while creating a harmonious balance between form and function.


Choose the perfect color palette to set the mood for your space. Our color consultation services guide you through the selection process, considering lighting, architecture, and your personal style to achieve a cohesive and inviting atmosphere.


Make a statement with bespoke furnishings and fixtures. Our team collaborates with skilled artisans to create unique, tailor-made pieces that enhance the character of your space.
